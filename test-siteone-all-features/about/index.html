About Me

I build startups well and prolifically – I’m probably best described as a serial startup CTO. For over two decades I’ve been creating tech companies. The result is an intersection of a broad range of skills: frontend, backend, web, mobile, databases, servers, UX, marketing, project management, hiring, and more. Tactically applying this combination results in an extremely efficient process that makes launching a startup easy to understand and a lot less risky.

I’m currently based in Colorado. My wife Lily and I enjoy reading, playing video games, and exploring the beautiful scenery of the Rocky Mountains. In my spare time I like to build experimental applications to explore new ideas and technologies (it’s a bit of an obsession).
